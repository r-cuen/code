Customer Service

We've all been there- that fustrating customer service call. Sometimes, it goes great! Your problem gets resolved right away! But it seems that, more often than not, everything that can go wrong, does goes wrong. Is it bad luck? Are you cursed with recieving terrible customer service? The truth is, the quality of a customer service experience all depends on the represenative. Sure, you're the one making the call, but their attitude guides the entire tone. This lesson serves as a guide to learn the first steps of providing quality customer service.
